Recipe Name (Khoresht Havij)

Other Names: Carrot Stew, Persian Stew

Type of Dish: Main Course

Main Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes

Optional Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Spinach

Short Description

This Persian stew, Khoresht Havij, is a flavorful blend of tender beef, carrots, and a mix of herbs, slow-cooked to perfection.

Recipe Stages (Short)

  1. Prepare the vegetables and meat.
  2. Cook the meat until tender.
  3. Add carrots, tomato paste, and spices.
  4. Simmer until everything is cooked together.
